The RedTim websites have a built-in data-driven analytics dashboard. You can get detailed reports of the search, social, and business inquiries/ leads. You will not need purchasing any other manufacturing website analytics tools.

Will my website visibility effect if I don’t update content?

Search engines recognize your website based on the content you update. If your website doesn't have fresh content, search engines will consider your site to be redundant. Multi-location business will need having a website for each location. Search engines recognize your website based on the keywords relevant to your business location. Basically, it helps you target traffic in your geography.
